Cheesy Pasta

  1. Serves 4
  2. V WF GF DF
  3. 15 mins prep 15 mins cook
  4. Main course
  5. Italian
  6. 150 kcal/serving

Ingredients

  1. Pasta 400g, 
  2. cheese 50g, 
  3. courgette 1, 
  4. tomato 4, 
  5. tuna 1 tin, 
  6. cumin pinch, 
  7. salt tspn, 
  8. pepper pinch.

Method

  1. Put pasta in boiling water
  2. Heat oil in another pan
  3. Add courgette and tomato
  4. Once cooked add pasta to tomatoes and courgette
  5. Mix in cheese
